LoL Friends

LoLSummoners is a new app to help you see how your friends are doing in the Fields of Justice. Adapting the material design to make it look really beautiful! The current version has the following functionality - Add summoners to follow from every region - See their Stats, Ranked Champion Stats and Recent Games (Detailed stats are due for a following release) - Vote for your favorite LCS players and Streamers, and find them in the Popular summoners tab. Future functionality includes: - Checking your friends live games - Details on every played game (Items, rivals, etc) - Mastery and Rune pages - More detailed summoner statistics

Screenshots and the full app available at:
